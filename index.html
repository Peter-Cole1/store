WHAT IS KOTAN?
Kotan is run by Rachael Kfare (@kafafka/@kotan)

Everyone’s hiding in some way. For a multitude of reasons, it always leads to a similar theme: protection. The mask is protection from your own thoughts or other people…from whatever it is you’re hiding from. Kotan is a project using my work which stems from feelings of anxiety and disconnect….as well as silly abstract thoughts.

Anyway-

It took some time to realize, and I even forget sometimes, but I’m not the only one who feels those feelings. My aim is to connect to others through Kotan so they know they aren’t alone either.

What are you hiding from?
